## English

A modern, high-performance, modular HUD (Heads-Up Display) for **Assetto Corsa**, inspired by the clean aesthetics of Sidekick and modern cyberpunk designs.

### Key Features
* **Modular Multi-Window Design**: Each widget (Speedometer, Gear & G-Force, Tires Status, Fuel, Lap Times, Debug Logs) is a separate floating window. You can drag and position them individually on your screen.
* **Cyberpunk Visuals**: Beautiful neon-glowing 192px icons for Speedometer, Gear, Fuel, Times, and Logs widgets, combined with sharp 1px vector borders and 8px corner brackets.
* **Segoe UI Typography**: Modern, crisp, and clean font rendering with strict left-aligned typography columns to eliminate text overlapping.
* **Highly Scalable**: Drag the options sliders in the HUD panel to scale individual widgets from **50% up to 400%** smoothly.
* **UAC Permission Safe**: Saves configurations and logs in the user's Documents/Assetto Corsa/cfg/apps/ folder, preventing any "Access Denied" or permission errors when installed in C:\Program Files (x86).
* **Clean Symmetrical Tires Widget**: Shows live tire temperatures, pressure deltas from ideal values, and brake temperatures centered inside the car chassis vector graphic.

### Installation
1. Download this repository.
2. Copy the pps folder into your Assetto Corsa root installation folder (e.g., C:\Program Files (x86)\Steam\steamapps\common\assettocorsa).
3. Open **Assetto Corsa** (or **Content Manager**) settings, go to **Apps**, and enable the checkmark next to **AlaschgariHUD**.
4. Once on track, hover your mouse over the right side of the screen, open the apps sidebar, and activate the AlaschgariHUD widgets you wish to display.
